Burton Ballet Academy's mission is to promote positive change in the lives of youth through creative movement and the arts. Our core purpose is to make dance fun, affordable, and accessible to all. In-studio classes are offered during the fall and spring term for ages 3 through adult in the following styles:
  • Ballet
  • Modern
  • Pointe
